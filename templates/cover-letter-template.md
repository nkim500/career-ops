# Cover Letter Template

Three moves, one page. No filler.

---

## Move 1 — Why you're here (1–2 sentences)

Not "I am applying for..." — the actual reason. What made you stop and apply to this specific company?
The hook should be genuine: a connection to the product, the problem, the mission. Something specific to them.

## Move 2 — What you've done that's relevant (2–3 sentences)

Two or three concrete things from your background that map directly to the work.
Not a CV summary — just the signal that makes them want to read your CV.

## Move 3 — What you want to do there (1 sentence)

Forward motion. What you want to contribute, not what you want to get.

---

## Rules

- No "I am excited to...", "I am passionate about...", or any variant
- No restating the JD back at them
- No filler transitions
- Reads like a note from someone who already understands the problem and has skin in the game
- One page max
